Top Two Most Anticipated Video Games of 2020

CyberPunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an upcoming role-playing video game developed and published by CD Projekt.

Adapted from the Cyberpunk franchise, the story takes place in dystopian Night City, an open world with six distinct regions. Players assume the first-person perspective of a customisable mercenary called V, who can acquire skills in hacking and machinery, an arsenal of ranged weapons, and options for melee combat.

Crash 4: It's about Time

Crash Bandicoot 4: It's About Time is an upcoming platform game developed by Toys for Bob and published by Activision.

t is the eighth main installment in the Crash Bandicoot series and the fourth chronologically, acting as a follow-up to the Crash Bandicoot N. Sane Trilogy, taking place directly after the events of Crash Bandicoot: Warped.
